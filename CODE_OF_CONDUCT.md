# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior includes:** Using welcoming and inclusive language, being respectful of differing viewpoints, gracefully accepting constructive criticism, focusing on what is best for the community, and showing empathy towards others.

**Unacceptable behavior includes:** Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, public or private harassment, publishing others' private information, and other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers directly. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
